# AI Solopreneur OS — the W_Counsel doctrine

> **Build your AI life without losing your soul.**
> 建立你的 AI 事业，守住你的灵魂。

This is the operating system for a one-person business in the age of AI. Not a course. Not a pile
of tools. Not anxiety. A **rhythm** — a way to think, build, ship, and stay yourself, every week,
with AI doing the heavy lifting.

It is built around one idea: **you don't need a team, you need a W_Counsel (军师) — a war-counsel.**
The legendary strategist 诸葛亮 (Zhuge Liang) never swung a sword. He read the ground, set the
formations, picked the moment, and held to a cause. That is what this OS is: your strategist, wired
into Claude Code, turning scattered effort into one campaign you can actually sustain.

---

## The shape of it

The OS has **five pillars (五柱)** — the surfaces you operate from day to day. The fourth
pillar holds the **doctrine: four loops (四谋)** you run every week.

```
五柱 — THE FIVE PILLARS
  势  Terrain          see the whole board            → the System Map
  律  Rhythm           your daily operating cadence   → the Daily Routine
  令  Morning Command  issue the day's orders         → Start the Day
  谋  Grand Strategy   the doctrine you run weekly     → the Four Loops (below)
  库  Arsenal          knowledge that arms you         → the Wiki → build · story · content

四谋 — THE FOUR LOOPS (inside 谋)
  知  Knowing      know yourself, know the field — kill noise before you move
  阵  Formation    build the structures that hold the line
  战  Campaign     wage the daily campaign — ship real work into the arena
  道  The Way      hold to your cause — the spine that keeps the rest sustainable
```

The loops are a **cycle, not a ladder.** You don't graduate from 知 to 道. You run all four,
every week. **道 (the Way) is the through-line** — it is what makes the other three last more than
a quarter.

## 四谋 — the four loops

### 知 · Knowing  *(was: Clarity)*
**Know yourself, know the field.** 知己知彼 — before you build anything, get clear: who you serve,
what you actually sell, what to ignore. Most solopreneurs are not behind; they just have no system
for deciding what matters. Knowing kills the noise first.

- The question it answers: *"What is actually worth my effort this week?"*
- Looks like: a sharp positioning line, one reframe, one decision made and logged — not ten tabs open.

### 阵 · Formation  *(was: System)*
**Build the structures that hold the line.** A second brain. Automations. Agents. The boring
infrastructure that means work still happens when you sleep, and you stop carrying everything in
your head.

- The question it answers: *"What did I have to redo this week that a system should have held?"*
- Looks like: one workflow captured, one automation wired, one agent that runs without you.

### 战 · Campaign  *(was: Output)*
**Wage the daily campaign.** Ship visible, real work — content, a product, a post, a small win —
into the arena where people can see it. Receipts, not plans. The campaign is where trust is earned.

- The question it answers: *"What did I put into the world this week that someone could react to?"*
- Looks like: something shipped every day, however small. Ugly and real beats polished and unposted.

### 道 · The Way  *(was: Soul)*
**Hold to your cause.** Protect your family, your values, your evenings, your name. The Way is your
moral ground — in classical strategy, 道 is the *first* of the five factors, because it is *why
people follow*. Lose it and the campaign wins battles but loses the war.

- The question it answers: *"Will this business still feel like mine in five years?"*
- Looks like: saying no to the anxiety play, keeping the 4:30 pickup, telling the true story.

## The weekly rhythm

The doctrine runs on a week. One default cadence (adapt it to your life):

| Day | Loop | What you do |
|-----|------|-------------|
| Mon | 阵 Formation | Build / fix one piece of the system |
| Tue | 知 Knowing | One mindset reframe, one positioning sharpening |
| Wed | 道 The Way | Tell one true story — no fake hooks |
| Thu | 战 Campaign | Ship something visible; show a real win |
| Fri | 阵 Formation | Wire one automation or agent |
| Sat | flagship | Distil the week into one canonical piece |
| Sun | — | Rest. The Way includes rest. |

The **Saturday flagship** is the one piece that compounds — the weekly artifact that, a year from
now, is an archive nobody can speed-run. Every durable solo creator has one.

## What this OS is *not*

It does not sell anxiety. It does not promise a monthly income. It does not invent client stories or
fake screenshots. It does not run on fake scarcity. (See [`operating-principles.md`](./operating-principles.md)
for the hard rules.) The register is **trusted, neutral, hands-on** — a counsel, not a guru.

> 我不卖焦虑，我装系统。 — *I don't sell anxiety. I install systems.*
