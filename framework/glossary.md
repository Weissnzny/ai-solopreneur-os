# Glossary — the canonical vocabulary

One name per idea. Use these exactly; don't swap synonyms mid-stream.

## The frame

| Term | 中文 | Means |
|------|------|-------|
| **AI Solopreneur OS** | AI 一人事业 OS | The whole system: a weekly operating rhythm for a one-person AI business. The product. |
| **W_Counsel / 军师** | 军师 | "War-counsel / strategist." The persona of the OS — your Zhuge Liang. Powers the optional voice+3D mascot. |
| **诸葛 / Zhuge** | 诸葛 | Zhuge Liang, the archetypal strategist. The face/avatar of the W_Counsel (the `zhuge.glb` model). |
| **军纪** | 军纪 | "Camp discipline." The operating principles that override everything (see `operating-principles.md`). |

## The Five Pillars — 五柱

| Pillar | 中文 | Pinyin | What it is |
|--------|------|--------|------------|
| The Terrain | 势 | shì | Seeing your whole OS at a glance — the lay of the land. (`/system-map`) |
| The Rhythm | 律 | lǜ | Your daily operating cadence. (`/start-day` + `/shutdown`) |
| The Morning Command | 令 | lìng | Issuing the day's orders — the kickoff. (`/start-day`) |
| The Grand Strategy | 谋 | móu | The doctrine: the four loops you run weekly. |
| The Arsenal | 库 | kù | Your knowledge base — what the OS routes into build, story, and content. (`wiki/`) |

## The Four Loops — 四谋

| Loop | 中文 | Pinyin | Was | What it is |
|------|------|--------|-----|------------|
| Knowing | 知 | zhī | Clarity | Know yourself, know the field; kill noise before you move. |
| Formation | 阵 | zhèn | System | Build the structures and systems that hold. |
| Campaign | 战 | zhàn | Output | Ship visible, real work into the arena, daily. |
| The Way | 道 | dào | Soul | Hold to your cause — family, values, the long game. The through-line. |

## Other terms

| Term | Means |
|------|-------|
| **The Arsenal routing** | The 库 pillar reads your knowledge vault and routes it three ways: **build** something, create a **story**, or create **content**. |
| **Saturday flagship** | The one canonical weekly piece that compounds trust over time. |
| **Vault** | The OS's own folder — the only place it writes freely. Everything else is read-only. |
