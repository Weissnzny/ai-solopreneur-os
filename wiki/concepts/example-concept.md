# Sell the outcome, not the tool (EXAMPLE — replace me)

## What it is
People don't buy your tool, service, or feature. They buy the after-state — the relief on the other
side. Frame and price around that, not around what you do. [[example-source]]

## How it works
1. Name the painful before — the concrete "2am moment".
2. Name the after-state in one sentence a tired person would nod at.
3. Make the offer the bridge between them; make the price reflect the after-state's value.
