# Pricing your first paid offer (EXAMPLE — replace me)

> A fictional example scenario, shipped to demonstrate a scenario page — the bridge from teaching to
> a real situation. Delete me and write your own real plays.

## The situation
You've helped a couple of people informally and want to make a first paid offer, but you don't know
what to charge or how to frame it.

## Concepts in play
- [[example-concept]] — sell the after-state, price to its value, not your hours.

## The play
1. Write the after-state in one sentence (the [[example-concept]] test).
2. Estimate what that after-state is worth to the buyer; price a clear fraction of it.
3. Deliver it small to a first cohort, prove it for one real person, then raise.
4. Stay honest: if you haven't delivered the after-state yet, say "first cohort" and price accordingly
   (`framework/operating-principles.md` — no fabrication).
